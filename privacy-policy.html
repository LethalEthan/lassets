Privacy policy

This privacy policy will be very simple, as of 30/06/2024 we do not store any information as of yet, it will change in the future and this page will be updated. LDBI Image manipulation is done all in memory and is discarded after edits, discord will store the images in their CDN however so make sure to read their privacy policy and terms of service. LDBI Voice transcription also does not store any voice recordings and is done in memory and discarded after transcription. Information in the future that will be stored as necessary will be user ID's, emails, guild names and ID's and possibly more and the privacy policy will be updated to reflect that. We will not sell or give away any information to third parties.

Thank you for using LDBI
